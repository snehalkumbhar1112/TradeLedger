📊 TradeLedger

TradeLedger is a complete business accounting and retail management software designed to streamline billing, inventory, and multi-location operations. It provides secure role-based access control for admins and users, ensuring efficient and controlled business workflows.

🔐 Role-Based Access Control
	Separate access for Admin and Users
	Secure and permission-based operations
🧾 Advanced Billing System
	Fast and accurate invoice generation
	Supports multiple sale types
🏬 Multi-Location Retail POS
	Manage multiple stores/branches seamlessly
	Location-wise stock tracking
📦 Inventory Management
	Real-time stock updates
	Inventory synchronization across locations
📊 Sales Analytics
	Insightful reports for business decisions
	Track performance across stores
🔍 Barcode Integration
	Quick product scanning
	Faster billing and stock handling

🧩 Core Modules
1. 🛒 Sales Module

Handles all sales operations with two types of sales:

APC Sale (At Purchase Cost)
Products are sold at their purchase price.
Regular Sale
Products are sold at their defined selling price.

2. 📥 Purchase Module
  Manage supplier purchases
  Update stock automatically upon purchase
  Maintain purchase history

3. 📦 Stock Module
  Track stock across multiple locations
  Maintain accurate inventory levels
  Real-time stock updates

🌐 Scalability
  Successfully supports 50+ retail chains
  Designed for high performance in multi-store environments
